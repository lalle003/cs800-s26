Summary

The cat (Felis catus), also called domestic cat and house cat, is a small carnivorous mammal. It is an obligate carnivore, requiring a predominantly meat-based diet.

Examples

Example 1

Its retractable claws are adapted to killing small prey species such as mice and rats.

Example 2

The domestic cat is the only domesticated species of the family Felidae.

See also

The origin of the English word cat comes from Old English catt, thought to derive from the Late Latin word cattus.
